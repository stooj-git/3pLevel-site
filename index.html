A Clean and
Easy-to-Use Inclinometer App

3pLevel is exactly what you were looking for: an inclinometer!

There are no fancy graphic elements or useless animations.

it's a dead simple and easy-to-use customisable inclinometer.

Customisable

Customise roll and pitch images of the inclinometer with the pictures of your own car (or the ones of your dream car).

Alarms

Keep on eye on the slopes with the inclinometer.

Set an alarm (Settings) to alert you when the inclination of your vehicle goes over a certain limit or it changes "too quickly" (alarms are available for both Roll and Pitch).
‍
In addition, 3pLevel can also tell you what was the maximum inclination during your session.

Universal App (Pro Version)

If you have an iPad, 3pLevel Pro is, our Universal App,  the inclinometer app for you.

Optimised for both iPhone and iPad, it's a must have.

Portrait and Landscape

3pLevel inclinometer can work on both landscape and portrait mode.

Do not forget to "lock" the app and reset the inclinometer before you start (while still on a flat terrain).

Colourful

Choose between 8 different colours, pick the one the marches better  your car, or your mood!

Inclinometers can be Fun

You can customise the 3pLevel inclinometer with any kind of image (not provided by the app), not just cars!

There are no limits.

Numbers or Images

Need a more "serious" and professional looking inclinometer, 3pLevel can be that too!

Disable the images from the Settings and here you are (you can still pick one of 8 the colours we selected for you).

Lot of Features

  • control your inclination (rolling and pitching)
  • measure the maximum inclination reached (rolling and pitching)
  • reset the maximum inclination (double tap on the inclinometer)
  • choose between images and degrees representation
  • customise the inclinometer views with your own car, or whatever car/image you like!
  • choose the colour that best matches your car or iPhone/iPad
  • works in both portrait and landscape mode
  • lock the screen orientation (landscape or portrait)
  • calibrate the inclinometer based on your current "inclination status" (single tap on the inclinometer)
  • set up visual and audio alarms based on inclination thresholds or rapid inclination variations
